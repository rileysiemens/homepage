Neapolitan Pizza Dough

Ingredients tweak

water
g
salt
g
instant yeast
g
bread flour
g

should between 55% - 63%

Steps

  1. Combine water and salt. Mix until dissolved. Then add yeast to mixture (Note: do not let yeast sit in salt mixture long. It will kill the yeast.)
  2. Add flour to mixture and mix until it forms a rough dough. Continue to knead until it forms a tight dough (around 10 minutes).
  3. Remove the dough from the mixing bowl and use your hands to form a tight dough ball. It should spring back when pressed.
  4. Lightly coat ball with olive oil, and place on a flat surface. Cover with plastic wrap, a cloth, or an upturned mixing bowl. Let it rest for 30 min.
  5. After resting, the dough should be much softer & stretchier. If it passes the window pane test it will make a good pizza.
  6. Divide into equal dough balls (~ g each). Put each ball into a lightly oiled, airtight container that will allow for some expansion (~1L containers work best).
  7. Store dough in the fridge for at least 8 hours, or up to 3 days.

  6. Divide into equal dough balls (~ g each). Put each ball into a lightly oiled, airtight container that will allow for some expansion (~1L containers work best).
